06 · Print Material Guide

Fingerprint smudges and paper grain only read as "real" on the right stock. Recommended for hobby printing:

BEST — SOFT-TOUCH LAMINATE
Velvet/soft-touch lamination over 300–350gsm cardstock. Grabs skin oils visibly, matches the smudge layer closely. Same finish as premium game/phone boxes.
GOOD — MATTE / UNCOATED CARDSTOCK
Plain matte 300gsm+ cardstock, no coating. Shows grain and light smudging naturally, easiest to source at local print shops.
AVOID — HIGH-GLOSS / HOLOGRAPHIC
Glossy UV coating and holographic laminate resist fingerprints (defeating the effect) and edge closer to authentic security-print finishes, which this tool avoids by design.
